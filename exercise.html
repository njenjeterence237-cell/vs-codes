London

London is the capital of England. It is the most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a matropolitan area of over 13million inhabitants.

Standing on the River Thames, London has been a major settlement for two millenia, its history going back to its founding by the Romans, who named it Londinium.

Douala

Douala is the chief metropolis of cameroon. It has a population of over 5million people. The seaport and the airport make Douala the main entry and exit town for people and goods.

As a Regional and Divisional Headquaters, The Governor of the Littoral and the SDO of the Wouri Reside here.
